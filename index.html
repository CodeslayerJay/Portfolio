And this is my story...

I became passionate about coding when I was fifteen years old. The year was 1997 and the PC game DOOM had just released source code to the public. Of course with my love for PC games, I became obsessed wanting to learn how to manipulate the game and eventually create my own. However just being a kid with no computer science degree, programming knowledge, and the lack of google, my only resources was a book titled, "Teach yourself C++ in 21 days", studying source code, and good ol' trial and error. I eventually was able to manipulate DOOM and other PC games, but never was able to make my own. That was the start I would spend the next twenty years on an amazing journey as a developer, a father, fitness fanatic, and self taught chef.
